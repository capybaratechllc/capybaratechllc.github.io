关于我们

About

Capybara Tech 专注“现场落地”。我们以清晰沟通、规范文档和可执行的交付节奏， 让弱电与网络工作按期完成，并且便于客户验收与运维。

Capybara Tech focuses on field execution. Clear communication, clean documentation, and a practical delivery cadence help low-voltage and network work finish on time—and remain easy to operate and maintain.

Delivery mindset: measurable, acceptable, traceable.
